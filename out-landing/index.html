Game modes
Six ways to play
Standard
Classic 8-player TFT. Full item pool, all nine generations, 100 HP. The definitive experience.
Hyper Roll
50 HP, cost 1–3 shop only. Fast, chaotic, reroll-heavy. A full game in under 20 minutes.
Legendary Clash
150 HP, cost 3–5 shop only. High-power units only — build the perfect late-game comp or lose slowly.
Nuzlocke
Any Pokémon that faints in combat is gone for the rest of the game. Every positioning decision counts.
Region Clash
Draft from a single generation with its exclusive signature item. Kanto, Johto, Hoenn — through Paldea.
Double Up
2v2 team mode. Share HP with your partner, trade items mid-game, and draft together against rival pairs.
