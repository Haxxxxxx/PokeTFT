What's inside
Everything TFT, built for Pokémon
Type Synergies
Stack Fire-types for burn damage, Electric for mana surges, Dragon for armor shred. 18 trait trees, each with multiple breakpoints that unlock unique combat behaviors.
Star Upgrades
Collect three copies to evolve into a two-star powerhouse. Time your spikes right and snowball before opponents can stabilise their boards.
Mega Evolutions
Equip a Mega Stone for a mid-fight transformation. Mega Charizard X, Mega Rayquaza, Mega Mewtwo — over 30 forms that reshape how a unit fights.
Item Crafting
Combine 68 items from 9 components. Spatulas forge trait emblems. Region Clash carousels feature the mode's signature completed item at high probability.
Augment Choices
Silver, Gold, and Prismatic augments reshape your strategy every game. 82 augments across three tiers — no two games play identically.
All 9 Generations
Kanto through Paldea — every generation fully represented including branching evolutions: Leafeon, Gallade, Appletun, Annihilape, and more.

Game modes
Six ways to play
Standard
Classic 8-player TFT. Full item pool, all nine generations, 100 HP. The definitive experience.
Hyper Roll
50 HP, cost 1–3 shop only. Fast, chaotic, reroll-heavy. A full game in under 20 minutes.
Legendary Clash
150 HP, cost 3–5 shop only. High-power units only — build the perfect late-game composition.
Nuzlocke
Any Pokémon that faints in combat is gone for the rest of the game. Every decision counts.
Region Clash
Draft from a single generation with its exclusive signature item. Kanto, Johto, Hoenn — through Paldea.
Double Up
2v2 team mode. Share HP with your partner, trade items mid-game, and draft together against rival pairs.
